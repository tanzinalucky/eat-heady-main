About Me

Any meal or beverage whose recipe has been changed to minimize fat, carbohydrates, and/or sugar in order to make it a component of a weight loss program or diet is referred to as a diet food (or dietetic food). Although bodybuilding supplements are made to make you gain weight, these foods are typically meant to help you lose weight or change the way your body looks.
